Popular Destinations

Kerala Backwaters

Experience the serene beauty of the backwaters in Kerala, with houseboats and lush greenery.

Hampi

Explore the ancient ruins of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site with stunning architecture.

Mysore Palace

Visit the grand Mysore Palace, known for its stunning architecture and vibrant history.

Meenakshi Temple

Admire the intricate architecture of the Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, a marvel of Dravidian architecture.

Goa Beaches

Relax on the beautiful beaches of Goa, known for their golden sands and vibrant nightlife.

Munnar

Enjoy the scenic beauty of Munnar, famous for its tea plantations and rolling hills.
